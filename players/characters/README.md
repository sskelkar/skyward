# Character Sheets

Choose one of the 8 director roles below. Each character sheet provides everything you need to build your department's systems.

---

## The 8 Director Roles

### 1. **Director of Customer Booking Portal** (`booking-portal.md`)
**Mission:** Build the customer-facing flight booking application

**What you'll build:**
- Flight search with filters
- Search results display
- Seat selection interface
- Booking confirmation flow

**Complexity:** Medium - User-facing, lots of UX polish needed
**Visual appeal:** High - Perfect for impressive demos
**Best for:** Engineers who enjoy frontend and UX

---

### 2. **Director of Flight Inventory & Scheduling** (`inventory-scheduling.md`)
**Mission:** Build flight schedule and seat inventory management

**What you'll build:**
- Flight schedule management

- Inventory dashboard

**Complexity:** Medium-High - Complex business logic

**Best for:** Engineers who enjoy backend and business logic

## How to Choose

### By Interest
- **Love frontend/UX?** → Booking Portal or Check-in & Boarding
- **Love backend/algorithms?** → Pricing or Inventory
- **Love ML/AI?** → Recommendations or Customer Service
- **Love analytics/data?** → Marketing or Pricing
- **Love real-time systems?** → Flight Status

### By Complexity
- **Easier:** Booking Portal, Check-in, Customer Service
- **Medium:** Inventory, Marketing
- **Harder:** Pricing, Recommendations, Flight Status

### By Visual Impact
- **Most impressive demos:** Flight Status (maps!), Check-in (boarding passes!), Recommendations
- **Great dashboards:** Pricing, Marketing, Inventory
- **User-facing polish:** Booking Portal, Customer Service

## What's in Each Character Sheet?

Every character sheet includes:

1. **Your Role** - Mission, motivations, and what keeps you up at night
2. **Your Department Architecture** - Recommended 2-3 service breakdown
3. **Quarter-by-Quarter Goals** - What to focus on each quarter
4. **Multi-Agent Strategies** - Specific patterns for your role
5. **Visual Demo Examples** - What to show in demos
6. **Common Challenges** - Role-specific pitfalls and solutions
7. **Integration Points** - How you connect with other departments (optional)
8. **Getting Started** - Pre-game prep and quarter summaries

---

## Quick Tips

- **Read your character sheet** before game day to get familiar with your department
- **All roles are equal** - no role is "better" than another, just different
- **Work independently** - departments use mock data by default, integration is optional
- **Build 2-3 services** - don't try to build everything, focus on quality demos
- **Use the guides** - Refer to `../TUTORIAL.md` for multi-agent patterns and `../DIRECTOR-GUIDE.md` for strategy
